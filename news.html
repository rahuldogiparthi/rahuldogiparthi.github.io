📅 March 2026 👤 Roy P et al.

New Study Identifies Disease-Associated Fibroblast Subpopulation in Human Heart Failure Using snRNA-seq

Our latest study identifies that Samd14 acts as a brake on excessive autophagy in anemia stress. These findings reveal a stress-dependent mechanism by which Samd14 sustains progenitor survival and ensures the production of healthy red blood cells during anemia.

iScience Read the paper →

NIH R01 Awarded for Spatial Transcriptomics Study of Pulmonary Arterial Hypertension

We are thrilled to receive a new NIH R01 grant from the NHLBI to investigate vascular lesion formation in PAH using spatial transcriptomics and cell-cell communication modeling over a 5-year award period.
